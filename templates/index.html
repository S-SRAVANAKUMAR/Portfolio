INAI.AI

INAI.AI is an intelligent, real-time multi-agent platform designed to enhance collaboration, productivity, and emotional well-being in team environments. Addressing the limitations of conventional tools, it introduces an adaptive layer that monitors conversations, task assignments, and behavioral cues to optimize team dynamics. The system uses Three agents: Synthesizer, Equalizer, and Uplifter to automate project updates, flag workload imbalances, and provide support during periods of stress or burnout. INAI.AI demonstrated strong results, including live summarization of team progress, fair workload redistribution, and proactive wellness checks, all seamlessly integrated via APIs and Azure OpenAI. It transforms teamwork into a dynamic, AI-guided experience that promotes clarity, fairness, and mental resilience.

Tools: Python, Azure OpenAI, REST APIs, Discord API, Multi-Agent Architecture, Communication Analytics

Fresh Start

Fresh Start is an award-winning web application built to ensure surplus food is redirected to those who need it most—rather than ending up in landfills. Developed during and crowned winner of StratX DevHacks 2025, the platform enables restaurants to securely register and upload food donations while allowing individuals to discover nearby food camps and free meal drives in real time. Featuring verified onboarding, live availability updates, and geolocation-based search, Fresh Start delivers a seamless and scalable solution for food redistribution. It transforms compassion into code—reducing waste, promoting sustainability, and supporting community well-being through technology.

Tools: Python, ReactJS, Flask, SQL Server, REST APIs, Google MAps APIs
